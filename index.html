Artistic Mastery

A home for artists, built around the table.

At Colette, two words guide every decision: artistic mastery. From our French-trained pastry chefs to the in-house artist whose abstract pieces line our walls, we put detail and comfort above everything else.

Surrounded by imported French coffee, authentic pastries, and bread crafted fresh every morning, you're transported to the romantic cobblestone streets of Paris — all while right here, in Alpharetta.

— Colette
